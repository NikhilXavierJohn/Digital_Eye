Know How

Built On Deep Learning
And Neural Networks

DIGITAL EYE is a free Online Tool That allows its users to find their lost or missing loved ones using facial identification and deep learning methodology.To know more about the library click here . The library is open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial.

Details

Users are required to upload 6 to 12 images (12+ recommended) of their loved ones and Their Names.The images are required to be potrait images or images that only have them in frame

Database

Images Are Securely stored along with the person's name and details. This is then sent to the servers for Encoding.

Process

Once Encoded Digital Eye Accurately Searches For Your Loved Ones Through The Cameras That are made available to the System throughout the City. Once A match Is found, Local Authorities are notified and the person is brought back to your home safely.
